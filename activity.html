MVJ ENIGEERING COLLEGE



MVJ College of Engineering was established in 1982 as the flagship institution of the Venkatesha Education Society to impart education in engineering, management, and beyond
. MVJCE's 15-acre campus in Whitefield, Bangalore, bears state-of-the-art infrastructure that creates an environment for progressive learning and development.

♥ PLACEMENTS

The Placement Cell at MVJCE facilitates between the corporate and the student for recruitment. More importantly, it is geared to understand corporate requirements and develop students who are employable.
. MVJCE has signed MoU with Seventh Sense People Development Solutions Pvt Ltd, Bengaluru, for providing training to our students.
Training programmes are designed to improve Personality Development, confidence levels, analytical thought, presentation delivery, communication skills, Basic & Advanced Aptitude skills and technical know-how.
MVJCE's Placement Cell is supported by group discussion rooms, interview rooms, seminar halls, and more than 600 computers with 2 Gbps internet and LAN connectivity that can be utilized for online tests.
